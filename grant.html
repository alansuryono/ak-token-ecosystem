AK Token currently operates on Polygon. This grant proposal focuses on exploring Sui-facing cultural NFT education, storytelling, community onboarding, and future utility experiments for the Sui ecosystem.

  • Design NFT utility and reward system prototype
  • Publish 10 educational articles or tutorials
  • Onboard 100 community members
  • Publish public progress updates through dashboard and website

3-Month Timeline
